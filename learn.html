hEDS — Hypermobile Ehlers‑Danlos Syndrome

A connective tissue condition that affects joints, ligaments, and more. This page collects approachable resources and day‑to‑day strategies that helped me.

MCAS — Mast Cell Activation Syndrome

A condition where mast cells release mediators too easily or too often. Triggers can be unpredictable; patterns and logging made the difference for me.

POTS — Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome

Standing becomes a workout. Salt, fluids, compression, and pacing are common tools. I share what was practical and what I wish I’d known sooner.
