About Me

I'm Beth—a storyteller, designer, and advocate. After years of unexplained decline in health, I finally received diagnoses that helped me understand what my body had been trying to say. This site is a record of that journey and a place to share what I’ve learned.

I believe in accessible information, small daily wins, and community. If my story resonates with you, I hope it offers light and a language for your own.

What you'll find here

  • Personal essays about navigating chronic illness
  • Plain-language explainers on hEDS, MCAS, and POTS
  • Tools, resources, and patterns that helped me
